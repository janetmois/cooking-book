Mix flour, baking soda, and salt. Pour into a bowl. Mix the softened butter, sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract in a large bowl until creamy. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Beat in the flour mixture in small portions (using a tablespoon). Mix thoroughly. Add chocolate chips (chopped nuts are also an option).
Using a rounded spoon, drop the mixture onto an unlined and ungreased baking sheet. Bake in a preheated oven at 375Â°F (190Â°C-200Â°C) for 9-12 minutes, until golden brown. Let cool for 2 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
