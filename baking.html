Sochniki

Sift the flour and baking soda. Combine the sour cream, sugar, and salt and mix thoroughly. Add the egg and beat with a mixer. Combine the softened butter with the sour cream and egg mixture. Add the sifted flour and knead the dough. Add a little more flour if necessary. Roll out the dough to a thickness of 7-8 mm. Cut out circles with a diameter of about 10 cm (a wide circle).
For the filling, beat the egg whites with sugar, then add the cottage cheese, sour cream, and flour. Place a teaspoon of the cottage cheese filling on each half of the dough, and then combine the halves. Brush the sochniki with beaten egg yolk. Bake in a preheated oven at 220Â°C for 30 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • For the dough:
    • 70 gr. softened butter
    • 4 tbsp sour cream
    • 2-2 1/2 cup flour
    • 1 egg
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 1/4 tsp baking soda
    • a pinch of salt
  • For the filling:
    • 170 g cottage cheese or curd mass
    • 2 tbsp sugar
    • 1 tbsp sour cream
    • 1 egg white
    • 1 tbsp flour
    • 1 egg yolk
