Pancakes

Warm the milk and add salt. Beat the eggs with sugar (not until foamy). Pour the eggs into the milk and gradually add the flour. The mixture should have the consistency of drinking yogurt, but not too thick. At the very end, add a little vegetable oil.
Grease a cold frying pan thoroughly with vegetable oil. Bake the pancakes in a well-heated frying pan, but not on the highest setting.

Ingredients:

  • 0.5 l milk
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 1/2-2 cup flour
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Syrniki

Mash the cottage cheese thoroughly, add the egg, sugar, and flour. Roll in flour and fry the syrniki over low heat.

Ingredients:

  • 0.5 kg cottage cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 0.5 cup sugar
  • 0.5 cup flour

Fluffy pancakes

Pour the kefir into a deep bowl, add the baking soda, let it sit for a few minutes, and then stir. Add the salt and sugar and stir. Gradually add the flour, sifting and mixing by hand (not with a mixer). You need to knead a thick dough. You can add vegetable oil.
Fry on medium power on both sides.

Ingredients:

  • 300 ml milk
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • a pinch of salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 180 g flour

French toast

Crack the egg into a deep bowl, add the sugar, and mix well. Pour in the milk and whisk until smooth. Soak each piece of bread thoroughly in the mixture.
Grease a cold frying pan with oil. Fry over medium heat on both sides.

Ingredients:

  • at least 4-6 pieces of bread
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 cup milk
